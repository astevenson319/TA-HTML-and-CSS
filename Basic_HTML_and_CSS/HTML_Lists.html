    Steps in Grocery Shopping

  1. Tabulate shopping list by viewing inventory in pantry, cupboard and refridgerator
  2. Acquire all shopping tools: wallet, shopping bags, etc.
  3. Transport self to Grocery Store
  4. Acquire shopping cart or basket as volume of target items will dictate
  5. Proceed with item acquisition, taking care and consideration for other store's customers, your fellow shoppers.
  6. Upon completion of item acquisition, queue at cash registers and exchange currency for items.
  7. Transport self to residence with items intact, and enjoy a successfully completed foray into Consumer Culture. Hooray for Civilization, Democracy and The Free Market System!
